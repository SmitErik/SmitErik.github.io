About me

Hi! I'm Erik Smit from Serbia, Vojvodina. Or you can call me $mitE for short. Yes, my nickname really came from my real name. Wow! So creative, I know! Whatever. Creativity is my middle name. As you can see in the names of my games.

I'm currently years old. I'm a third-year student in the Faculty of Science and Informatics at the University of Szeged.

My hobby of making games started when I was 15. My elementary school computer science teacher introduced me to this world. The first 6-7 games were created in that spring and summer. After that, in high school I made one more game about shooting my classmates. They really enjoyed it. Except the teacher... But moving on, I made my first ever 'big' game called 'The Dark Library'. I was pretty proud of myself. I also went to a competition with it. I saw many other games in the competition, some of which were made in Unity. I liked the game engine right away. So I started turning to Unity and to the 3D games.

First I became friends with the engine and the whole 3D stuff. I made my first 3D game called 'HalloBab' (bab means bean in hungarian). Just a silly random name. The main character looked like a bean - hence the name. It wasn't anything special. It isn't even finished. I put it here on my website, because why not? Here's my second game too, called 'Game'. I said, that I'm such a creative guy. But after these little clumsiness, I started creating my masterpiece.

'Logic'. It is the work of my life. I am proud to say that I made this game. From the beginning to the end. But unfortunately it hasn't got an end yet. I still need to work on it. After 3 long years (with breaks here and there) I still haven't finished it... That's pretty sad. But in my defense, I had a lot of other stuff to do too. Eg school, university, etc... Okay, it's true that I bit off more than one can chew... But still... It should be finished sometime.

So that's why I decided to bring myself and continue to work on 'Logic'. It has to be done. Once I start, I have to finish it. So in the near future if you see that the patch list is not changing, contact me somehow. Here are some my contacts:
